References

A reference refers to a value at a point in time, and acts as an identity. Your bank account balance is a reference, but at any point in time it has a specific, immutable value.

References can be observed in a generic way, without requiring coordination with other parties. This implies that all references guarantee atomic transitions between values. Some references may make additional concurrency-related promises as well.
